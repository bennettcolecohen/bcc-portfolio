Robust, ML-powered sports betting engine with 272% real ROI.

BetML is a set of three separate projects (BetNBA, BetMLB, and BetNFL [WIP]), which combine supervised learning and classical decision theory to place optimal sports bets to maximize profit over time. BetML introduces more effective strategies to consider risk than what traditional risk aversion functions offer. The project also improves upon the Kelly Criterion investment sizing strategy to include a time delay on bet scaling to make it more resilient against large swings and variance.

BetNBA was tested last season and profited over 272% in real, legal betting environments over four months. Each model is built with its own nuances from the sport itself, such as BetMLB using exclusively player features instead of team features as the lineups and batting order change every day. The first iteration began in Fall 2020, and I now offer most of the models' recommended bets at the link above. I have written a few academic papers/reports and blogs on the topics, and am looking to publish a complete paper on the methods and intricacies in the near future.
