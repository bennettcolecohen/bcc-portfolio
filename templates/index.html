About Me

Hey, I'm Bennett Cohen, and I love solving problems with machine learning. By day, I work as a part-time ML engineer and software developer, integrating ML and data science into projects for clients across financial, legal, and real-estate sectors. By night, I'm finishing my Masters of Science at the University of California, Berkeley, specializing in Applied Machine Learning & Optimization through the Department of Industrial Engineering & Operations Research (shortened to just IEOR). I received my Bachelors of Science in IEOR from UC Berkeley as well, with a minor in Data Science.

When I'm not staring at a computer screen, you can find me snowboarding in Lake Tahoe, working out with my friends, or cooking dinner for my family. Before and during college, I spent a few years as a freelance graphic designer, designing websites and brand identities for clients and startups. I was actually going to go to art school before I landed in engineering.

I'm looking to apply ML techniques and optimization to address issues in our world like education inequality, climate change, and interpersonal connection/mental health. If you're looking for a one-off job, hiring for a full-time, or simply want to chat about the state of the world, freel free to reach out at bennettcohen@berkeley.edu.
